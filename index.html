NitroSteel

A longer-lasting alternative to chrome plating, NitroSteel/FNC is a ferritic nitrocarburized bar that is wear-resistant and corrosion-resistant. It supplies durability and leaves no hazardous byproduct in our facility or your operations.

Why NitroSteel?

Superior Performance

Superior corrosion resistance means no pitting and flaking, unlike chrome.

Increased Profitability

Wear resistance ensures a durable and long-lasting product that keeps your equipment out of the shop and in the field.

Environmentally Safe

Unlike chrome, NitroSteel leaves no hazardous byproduct in our facility or in your operations.
